A different perspective

A different perspective

Over the years, we’ve been trying to come up with the best way to say, “Hello, we’re Neptune, and this is
who we are.” It’s easy to learn that we’re an interiors brand – our stores and our website will tell you that
in no time. But while our designs are a huge part of who we are, ‘the why’ and ‘the how’ almost matter
more than ‘the what’. It’s easy to tell you what we do, it’s harder to explain why. That’s where our
philosophy, a different perspective, comes in. Three words that sum up our entire reason for being.
